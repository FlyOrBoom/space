How to play
Set is a simple card game. Its cards have four features: shape, color, shade, and number.
You earn points if you find a "set" — a group of three cards which must satisfy all of the following criterias:
· The shape of the symbols on the selected cards must all be different or all be the same.  
· The shades of the symbols on the selected cards must all be different or all be the same.
· The colors of the symbols on the selected cards must all be different or all be the same.  
· The numbers of symbols on the selected cards must all be different or all be the same.
